Number Nexus

Game Rules & How to Play

  • Guess the hidden number in the displayed range.
  • You have 5 guesses and 2 minutes to find it.
  • First hint is shown immediately after game starts.
  • Second hint after your first guess.
  • Third hint after your second guess.
  • "Too high"/"Too low" feedback is shown above the guessing box for your first 3 guesses.
  • Only winning scores appear on the leaderboard.
